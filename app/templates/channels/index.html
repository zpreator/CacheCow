Subscriptions

How Subscriptions Work

These are app-specific subscriptions, separate from your YouTube account. Add channels by URL and CacheCow will periodically download new videos from them.

Why Limit Downloads?

Download limits help manage disk space. By default, only recent videos within a certain number of days and up to a maximum count are downloaded.
